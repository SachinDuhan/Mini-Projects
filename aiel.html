We are the Aiel, a proud and ancient people who call the Aiel Waste our home. Our heritage is one of strength, resilience, and unwavering devotion to our honor code, the ji'e'toh. Our expertise lies in the art of survival in the harsh desert environment, where we have developed unparalleled skills in tracking, hunting, and hand-to-hand combat. We are masters of the spear, and our warriors are renowned for their deadly accuracy and speed.
